# Add your traffic footage here

No sample footage is bundled. Add footage only when you have the right to use it.

Good test coverage should include fixed-camera videos with:

- approaching and departing cars at several distances;
- plates large and sharp enough to read in multiple frames;
- a measured road segment or four surveyed road-plane calibration points;
- known speed limit and route/camera name;
- lanes, stop line, traffic-light region, and parking zones visible when those rules are tested;
- ordinary non-incident footage to measure false positives.

Prefer 1080p or better, stable framing, daylight before night footage, and the original file rather than a social-media recompression. TrafficVision accepts MP4, MOV, AVI, and MKV through the application. Do not place personally identifiable footage in a public repository.
